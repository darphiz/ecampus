Practice all Past Questions here on ecampus.edu.ng and blast your upcoming exam!. The simulator is useful for every student especially 1st-year students because most of their exams are Computer Based Test.
For a wonderful experience, it's recommended you use a chrome browser.

There are 2 modes available for now

1. The quiz mode

The answer and explanation to the question are shown after answering it and performance is shown after ten attempts. This mode is not time-dependent.

2. The CBT mode

This is like the real exam, answers and explanations are shown at the end of the exam. It is time-dependent, as the time counts down from 20minutes. The exam is automatically submitted when the time reaches 0!
Discipline yourself to practice at least 10 CBT tests right here every day, by doing this you get rid of exam phobia and learn proper time management.
